Chess Caffeinated

History


Chess Caffeinated was opened in 1972 by Morbius. It was established after Bobby Fischer used his morbing abilities to defeat reigning world champion Boris Spassky. Since then it has become a hotspot of chess enthusiasts, as well as being a host to many chess related tournaments such as the Sinkhole Cup and the MeltH20 Championship.

About


Chess Caffeinated is one of the cafes of all time. Truly, this is where the fun begins. Our cafe features multiple chess tables with designated tables for drinks, as well as free wifi connection so you can tweet about your win on our social media. We also included checkers for the disabled or mentally challenged people.

Chess and Coffee


Our menu features many types of coffee and desserts. It ranges from cappucinos, lattes, macchiatos, to cheesecakes and carrot cakes. We believe that chess and coffee go together because a fully replenished player makes for a better competition. We also prioritize our customers' safety by making sure to sanitize each of the chess boards after players have finished their turns.
